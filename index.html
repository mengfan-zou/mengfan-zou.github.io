Digital Communication · Game Studies · Media Literacy

Hi, I’m Mengfan Zou.

I study how digital technologies, video games, online communities and AI-mediated communication shape literacy, agency, identity and cultural participation.

I am a communication researcher based in Madrid, with a PhD in Communication, Information and Technology in the Network Society. My work connects digital communication, game studies, media literacy, disinformation, gender and online communities, with a strong interest in translating research into education, academic consulting and digital strategy.
